About

Welcome to Nature's Hideaway, a premier glamping site nestled on the breathtaking west coast of Ireland. Our tranquil retreat offers an array of accommodations, including cosy bell tents, luxurious geodesic domes, and charming wooden A-frame cabins, each designed to provide a perfect blend of comfort and rustic charm.
Set amidst lush landscapes with stunning Atlantic Ocean views, Nature's Hideaway invites you to disconnect from the hustle and reconnect with nature. Explore scenic coastal trails, relax on pristine beaches, or enjoy a peaceful evening by the campfire under a canopy of stars.
Whether you're seeking adventure or tranquility, Nature's Hideaway promises an unforgettable escape, offering a serene haven where the beauty of the Irish coast meets modern luxury.

Why Stay With Us

At Nature's Hideaway on Ireland's west coast, guests can immerse themselves in a variety of activities. Explore scenic hiking trails that meander through lush landscapes and offer breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean.
Take a refreshing swim at one of the nearby beaches, perfect for a morning dip or a leisurely afternoon. Enhance your relaxation with yoga classes held in a serene outdoor setting, promoting mindfulness and well-being.
Whether seeking adventure or tranquility, Nature's Hideaway offers the perfect blend of outdoor excitement and peaceful retreat.
